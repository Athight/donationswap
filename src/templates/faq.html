Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

TL;DR: Donations to effective charities are not tax-deductible in all countries. With this website, you can find people who live in countries with better tax laws, and swap donations with them.

Does charity even work?

Oh yes. If chosen well. That's what Effective Altruism is all about. Watch William MacAskill's TED talk for a great introduction to the subject.

Is it free?

Absolutely. Both in the sense of "free beer" and in the sense of "free speech".

100% of your donation goes to charity. We don't take a cut, we don't charge for our services. We also don't sell your email address or use it for anything other than sending you an email when we've found a matching donor.

Even the software that drives this server is free. You can check it out at https://github.com/donationswap/donationswap/.

What's in it for you?

The warm fuzzy feeling that we've helped to make the world a better place, by routing money from the tax department to effective charities.

Does this website work with Internet Explorer?

No, it does not.

Why not?

Internet Explorer is notoriously hard to write code for. It does not support JavaScript classes, promises, or even the lambda operator. With a market share of around 3% (according to Wikipedia), it's just not worth it to use outdated coding practices just to make the website work in Internet Explorer.
